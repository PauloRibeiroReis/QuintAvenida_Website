The Project

Live longer, live better,
in community

Vision — To create a self-sustaining rural ecosystem in Portugal where active seniors thrive together — blending technology, nature and genuine human connection into a new model of cooperative living.

Mission — To develop a productive estate with shared housing and services, offering an active ageing experience in the Portuguese countryside with high standards of comfort, healthcare and cutting-edge technology.

Strategy

The four pillars of the project

01
🏘️

Community Hub

Development & Governance

The cooperative framework that defines rules, group cohesion and the legal and financial sustainability of the community.

  • Housing cooperative structure
  • 15–20 modular ecological homes
  • Democratic decision-making
  • Shared recurring income

🌿

Hospitality & Agritourism

Revenue Generation

The economic engine of the project — boutique farm-to-table experiences, nature retreats and authentic Portuguese rural tourism.

  • 6 boutique guest suites
  • Professional kitchen · km 0
  • Farm experiences & activities
  • Mid-to-high-end authentic market
03
🤖

Health & Technology

Differentiation & Quality of Life

AI, robotics and telemedicine woven into daily life for healthy, monitored longevity — technology that cares, not intrudes.

  • AI-powered smart wearables

  • Mindfulness & nutrition retreats
  • Adapted gym & yoga studio

🤝

Innovation

Nature and Technology
in harmony

Physical Components

Infrastructure designed for
community living

🏛️

Central Building

6 guest suites, professional kitchen, multipurpose hall, gym, swimming pool, treatment rooms and industrial laundry.

🏡

Residential Cluster

15–20 independent timber/CLT homes, A+ energy rated, adapted for senior mobility. Ground floor, barrier-free design.

☀️

Energy Autonomy

Solar photovoltaic panels and community batteries. Smart home control via app and full Wi-Fi for telemedicine.

🥬

Organic Garden

Certified organic farming for own consumption, km 0 restaurant supply and sharing with the local community.

🚲

Green Mobility

Electric bicycles and shared senior-adapted buggies. Shared car fleet with minimal carbon footprint.

💻

Operations Centre

Administrative hub for tourism, retreats and home-care coordination. Bookings, scheduling and route management.
